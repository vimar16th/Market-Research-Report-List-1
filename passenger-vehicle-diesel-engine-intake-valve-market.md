<p><p>The intake valve market for passenger vehicle diesel engines can be categorized into three types: mono metallic valves, bimetallic valves, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made of a single material, such as steel or titanium. Bimetallic valves are composed of two different materials, usually steel and a heat-resistant alloy. Hollow valves have a hollow stem, which helps in reducing the valve weight and improving performance. Each type of valve has its own benefits and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>

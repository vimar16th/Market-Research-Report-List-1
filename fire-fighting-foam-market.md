<p><p>Firefighting foam is a crucial tool in effectively combating fires. There are three main types of firefighting foam: protein foam, synthetic foam, and Class A foam. </p><p>Protein foam is derived from animal protein and forms a thick, stable blanket that suppresses oxygen supply to a fire. Synthetic foam is made from synthetic detergents and offers excellent fire extinguishing capabilities as it creates a film on the burning surface. Class A foam is specifically designed for extinguishing fires involving solid combustible materials such as wood and paper by reducing the surface tension of water, aiding in penetration and extinguishment.</p></p>

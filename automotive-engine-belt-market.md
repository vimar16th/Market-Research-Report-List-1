<p><p>The automotive engine belt market consists of various types of belts, namely rubber, PVC, Neoprene, and CR. Rubber belts, known for their flexibility and durability, are widely used in vehicles due to their resistance to heat and wear. PVC belts, made of polyvinyl chloride, offer excellent resistance to chemicals and corrosion. Neoprene belts, made of synthetic rubber, are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles due to their superior strength and resistance to oil and heat. CR belts, made of chloroprene rubber, provide similar properties to neoprene belts but are more cost-effective.</p></p>

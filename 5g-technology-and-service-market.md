<p><p>5G Technology and Service encompass three market types: Enhanced Mobile Broadband (eMBB), Ultra-Reliable Low-Latency Communications (URLLC), and Massive Machine-Type Communications (MMTC). eMBB focuses on providing higher data speeds and capacity for activities like HD video streaming and augmented reality/virtual reality applications. URLLC offers ultra-reliable and low-latency connectivity crucial for mission-critical services such as autonomous vehicles and remote healthcare. MMTC targets massive machine connectivity for the Internet of Things, connecting a vast number of low-power devices for smart cities and industrial automation. These market types highlight the diverse capabilities that 5G aims to provide.</p></p>

<p><p>The PCB cleaner market can be categorized into three major types: solvent-based, water-based, and others. Solvent-based cleaners contain chemical solvents, such as alcohol, acetone, or petroleum-based compounds, which effectively dissolve and remove contaminants from PCBs. Water-based cleaners, on the other hand, use water as a base along with surfactants and detergents to dislodge and clean the PCBs. The "others" category typically includes alternative cleaning methods, such as ultrasonic or vapor cleaning, which utilize different techniques but aim to achieve similar results of effectively removing contaminants from PCBs.</p></p>

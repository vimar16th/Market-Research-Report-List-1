<p><p>Automotive electric motors for EV can be divided into two major types: AC motors and DC motors. AC motors, or alternating current motors, are commonly used in electric vehicles due to their high power efficiency and smooth operation. They are driven by alternating current and allow for regenerative braking. On the other hand, DC motors, or direct current motors, are simpler in design and less expensive. They run on direct current and are widely used in hybrid electric vehicles as well as some all-electric vehicles.</p></p>

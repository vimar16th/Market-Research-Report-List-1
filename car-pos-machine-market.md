<p><ul><li>Wired</li><li>Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car POS Machine market can be divided into two types: wired and wireless. Wired POS machines require a physical connection to a network or system, typically through cables or wires. They are commonly used in fixed locations such as car service centers or dealerships. On the other hand, wireless POS machines operate without any physical connections and use wireless networks, allowing for more mobility and flexibility. These are often used in car rental services or mobile repair units that require on-the-go transactions.</p></p>

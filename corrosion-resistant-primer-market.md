<p><p>Corrosion-resistant primer is a protective coating used to prevent corrosion on various surfaces. There are two types available in the market: single component and multiple component. Single component primers are ready-to-use and require no mixing, making them easier to apply. On the other hand, multiple component primers consist of two or more separate components that must be mixed before application. They are typically used in more demanding environments where higher corrosion resistance is necessary. Both types play an important role in protecting surfaces from corrosion and extending their lifespan.</p></p>

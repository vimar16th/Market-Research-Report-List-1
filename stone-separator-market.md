<p><p>The stone separator market encompasses two main types: Trailed and Rotary separators. Trailed stone separators are mobile machines that can be attached to tractors for easy transportation and operation. They are designed to remove stones and debris from agricultural fields, enhancing soil quality and preventing damage to machinery during cultivation. On the other hand, Rotary stone separators feature rotating drums that effectively separate stones and trash from the soil. These machines are ideal for larger-scale operations, as they can cover more ground and process a higher volume of materials in a shorter time frame.</p></p>

<p><p>The concrete containing polymers market is divided into several types. Polymer Concrete (PC) is a blend of polymers and aggregates, providing enhanced strength and durability. Latex-modified Concrete (LMC) involves adding latex polymer to the mix, improving flexibility and reducing cracking. Polymer-impregnated Concrete (PIC) is created by impregnating a polymer into the concrete to enhance its properties, such as waterproofing. Lastly, the "others" category includes different forms of polymer-enhanced concrete, like fiber-reinforced concrete or polymer-cement concrete, offering unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>

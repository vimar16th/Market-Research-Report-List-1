First is Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC), which uses wireless communication between trains and trackside equipment. Positive Train Control (PTC) enables real-time monitoring and control of trains, improving safety and reducing collisions. Automatic Train Control (ATC) systems regulate train movements and prevent accidents. These signaling systems are essential for enhancing railway safety and capacity while maintaining reliable and punctual train services.</p></p>

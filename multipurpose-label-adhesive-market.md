<p><p>The Multipurpose Label Adhesive Market includes two main types: Water-based Label Adhesive and Solvent-based Label Adhesive. Water-based adhesives are made by dispersing polymer particles in water, providing an eco-friendly and low-toxicity option. Solvent-based adhesives, on the other hand, are produced by dissolving a polymer in an organic solvent. This type offers faster drying times and stronger bonding properties. Both types are widely used in various industries, such as packaging, retail, and manufacturing, for labeling purposes due to their versatility and effectiveness.</p></p>

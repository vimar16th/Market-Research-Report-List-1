<p><p>Electro-Pneumatic Single Acting Positioners are a type of positioners used in industrial applications to control the position of pneumatic actuators. They convert electrical signals into pneumatic pressure, allowing for precise control and positioning of the actuator. On the other hand, Electro-Pneumatic Double Acting Positioners are also used in industrial settings but provide control for both the opening and closing motion of the actuator. These positioners use electrical signals to regulate both the positive and negative pressure to ensure accurate positioning of the actuator.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive steering knuckle is a vital component of the steering system that connects the wheels and the suspension system. It plays a crucial role in controlling the direction of the vehicle during cornering and ensures smooth steering operation. The steering knuckle is responsible for bearing the entire weight of the vehicle, along with handling forces from uneven road surfaces and turning motions. It is typically made of cast iron or aluminum, providing high strength and durability.</p><p>Market Analysis:</p><p>The global automotive steering knuckle market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing production and sales of automobiles, particularly passenger cars, are driving the demand for steering knuckles. This has led to the development of advanced materials and design technologies, resulting in enhanced fuel efficiency and reduced environmental impact.</p><p>Latest Trends:</p><p>One of the key trends in the automotive steering knuckle market is the integration of advanced technologies such as electric power steering (EPS) systems. This trend is particularly prominent in electric and hybrid vehicles.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing adoption of lightweight materials such as aluminum alloys.

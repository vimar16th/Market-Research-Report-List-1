<p><p>Composite testing is utilized to assess and validate the quality and performance of various types of composites in the market. Polymer Matrix Composites involve testing composites made of two or more materials, with a polymer matrix. Continuous Fiber Composites use long fibers, like carbon or glass, embedded in a matrix for enhanced strength. Ceramic Matrix Composites consist of fibers embedded in a ceramic matrix and display superior thermal resistance. Discontinuous Fiber Composites employ short fibers, boosting resistance to cracking. Other composites may include metal matrix composites or bio-based composites, among others.</p></p>

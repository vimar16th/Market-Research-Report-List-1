<p><p>The Automotive Gudgeon Pin market consists of three main types: fixed pin, full floating pin, and semi-floating pin. In the fixed pin type, the pin is fixed in both the connecting rod and piston, providing excellent stability. Conversely, the full floating pin allows free movement between the connecting rod and piston, reducing friction and enhancing engine performance. The semi-floating pin combines the benefits of both types, with limited movement to reduce friction while maintaining stability. Each market type caters to different engine specifications and requirements.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Meltblown</li><li>Spunbonded</li><li>Spunlace</li><li>Needle Punch</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-woven fabric market consists of various types such as Meltblown, Spunbonded, Spunlace, Needle Punch, and Others. Meltblown fabric is created through a process of blowing melted polymer fibers, offering excellent filtration properties. Spunbonded fabric is manufactured by bonding continuous filaments together, delivering strength and durability. Spunlace fabric is produced by entangling fibers through high-pressure water jets, resulting in a soft and absorbent material. Needle Punch fabric involves mechanically interlocking fibers, providing excellent insulation and soundproofing properties. The "Others" category includes various non-woven fabric types with distinctive characteristics not covered by the aforementioned.</p></p>

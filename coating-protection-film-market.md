<p><p>Coating Protection Film (CPF) market consists of three main types: PVC Type, PU Type, and TPU Type. PVC Type CPF is made of polyvinyl chloride and widely used due to its affordability and easy application. PU Type CPF is produced using polyurethane and offers high transparency and durability, making it suitable for automotive and electronic applications. TPU Type CPF utilizes thermoplastic polyurethane, providing excellent abrasion resistance, flexibility, and chemical resistance. These CPF types are protective films used to shield surfaces from scratches, impacts, and other forms of damage.</p></p>

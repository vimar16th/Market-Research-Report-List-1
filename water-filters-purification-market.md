<p><p>Water filters and purification market types include reverse osmosis and ultrafiltration membrane systems. Reverse osmosis is a process that uses pressure to force water through a membrane, removing impurities, minerals, and contaminants. Ultrafiltration membrane works similarly but uses a smaller pore size to filter out bacteria, viruses, and larger particles. Both types of systems provide effective water purification by eliminating harmful substances, ensuring clean and safe drinking water for households and industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Granular sulphur refers to a solid form of sulfur that is widely used as a raw material in various industries including agriculture, chemical manufacturing, and pharmaceuticals. It is a bright yellow crystalline solid that is odorless and insoluble in water. Granular sulphur is primarily obtained through the Claus process where hydrogen sulfide gas is burned to produce elemental sulfur, which is then cooled and solidified into granules.</p><p>The granular sulphur market is witnessing significant growth due to its extensive applications in the production of fertilizers, pesticides, and sulfuric acid. The increasing demand for agricultural products and the rising focus on improving crop yields are driving the market growth. Additionally, the growing use of granular sulphur in rubber vulcanization, sugar refining, and metal ore processing industries is further fueling the market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the market is expected to be positively influenced by the increasing utilization of granular sulphur in the oil and gas industry for hydrogen sulfide removal and as a refining agent. The stringent environmental regulations related to sulfur emissions and the need for desulfurization in petroleum refining are propelling the demand for granular sulphur.</p><p>According to market analysis, the granular sulphur market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.7% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by factors such as the rising population and the subsequent need for increased food production, as well as the growing demand for industrial chemicals. Additionally, the expansion of the oil and gas industry and the increasing adoption of granular sulphur in the pharmaceutical sector are expected to further boost the market in the coming years.</p></p>

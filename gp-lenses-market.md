<p><p>GP lenses, also known as gas-permeable or rigid gas-permeable lenses, are a type of contact lens made from a durable material that allows oxygen to pass through to the cornea. These lenses have a lower water content compared to soft lenses, which helps to reduce the risk of bacterial contamination and allows for better comfort and healthier eyes.</p><p>The GP lenses market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. This can be attributed to various factors such as an increasing prevalence of vision disorders, growing awareness about eye care, technological advancements in lens materials, and a rising number of people opting for contact lenses over traditional eyeglasses.</p><p>Additionally, GP lenses offer several advantages over soft lenses, including better vision correction, increased durability, reduced risk of eye infections, and superior oxygen permeability. These factors are driving the adoption of GP lenses among both patients and eye care professionals.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing the emergence of innovative lens designs and materials that enhance comfort, ease of use, and optical performance.

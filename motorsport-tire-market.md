<p><strong>The Motorsport Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorsport tire market consists of two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have layers of cords running radially from the center of the tire to its outer edges. This design provides better stability, performance, and heat dissipation, making them commonly used in high-speed racing. On the other hand, bias tires have layers of cords running diagonally from bead to bead. They are more durable and suitable for rough terrains or off-road racing due to their ability to handle higher loads and resist punctures.</p></p>

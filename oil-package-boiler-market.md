<p><strong>The Oil Package Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>D-type Package Boilers</li><li>A-type Package Boilers</li><li>O-type Package Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Oil package boilers are categorized into three types:</p><p>1. D-type Package Boilers: These boilers have a large steam and water drum at the top and a series of smaller water tubes extending from the drum to the lower headers. The water tubes are inclined to enhance heat transfer, making them suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature industrial applications.</p><p>2. A-type Package Boilers: These boilers have a smaller steam and water drum at the top and larger water tubes extending from the drum to the lower headers. This design allows for a faster response to load changes and is commonly used in utility and industrial applications.</p><p>3. O-type Package Boilers: These boilers have two water drums located at the top and bottom, with a steam drum located in the middle. The design promotes efficient combustion and heat transfer and is commonly used in oil refinery and petrochemical plant applications.</p></p>

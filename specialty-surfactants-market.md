<p><p>The specialty surfactants market is segmented into four types: anionic surfactants, cationic surfactants, amphoteric surfactants, and nonionic surfactants. Anionic surfactants have a negative charge and are widely used in detergents and cleaning products. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge and are utilized in fabric softeners and hair conditioners. Amphoteric surfactants can carry both positive and negative charges, making them versatile in various applications. Nonionic surfactants have no charge and are preferred for their low skin irritation, making them suitable for personal care products and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

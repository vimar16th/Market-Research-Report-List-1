<p><p>The multi-rotor drone market can be categorized into three types based on the number of rotors: three rotor drone, four rotor drone, and six rotor drone. A three rotor drone, also known as a tricopter, offers agility and simplicity in design. A four rotor drone, commonly called a quadcopter, is the most popular and widely used type known for its stability and ease of control. Lastly, a six rotor drone, referred to as a hexacopter, provides increased payload capacity and improved redundancy for applications that require heavy lifting or enhanced reliability.</p></p>

There are two main types of these separators: self-cleaning and manual cleaning. Self-cleaning separators have a mechanism that automatically removes trapped magnetic materials using a belt or a rotating drum. On the other hand, manual cleaning separators require human intervention to physically remove the captured magnetic materials. Both types are used based on specific needs and application requirements in different industries.</p></p>

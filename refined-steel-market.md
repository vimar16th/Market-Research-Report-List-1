<p><p>The refined steel market encompasses three main types: Special Quality Carbon Steel, Low Alloy Steel, and Alloy Steel. Special Quality Carbon Steel refers to steel with a higher carbon content, making it strong, durable, and suitable for various industrial applications. Low Alloy Steel contains small amounts of alloying elements, enhancing its strength and corrosion resistance. Alloy Steel contains higher amounts of alloying elements such as chromium, nickel, or molybdenum, giving it superior properties like increased strength, hardness, and heat resistance. These different types cater to diverse requirements in industries like construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>

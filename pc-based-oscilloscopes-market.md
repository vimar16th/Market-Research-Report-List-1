<p><p>PC-based oscilloscopes are electronic test instruments that are used to visualize and analyze signals and waveforms. Firstly, the increasing adoption of advanced technologies such as IoT, telecommunications, and automotive electronics has led to a rising demand for precise signal visualization and measurement tools. PC-based oscilloscopes offer high-speed data acquisition and analysis capabilities, making them ideal for these applications.</p><p>Moreover, the growing trend of miniaturization and portability in electronic devices has further boosted the demand for PC-based oscilloscopes. These instruments are compact and lightweight, making them easy to carry and use in various field applications.</p><p>Additionally, the integration of advanced features such as real-time spectrum analysis, digital filtering, and waveform math functions in PC-based oscilloscopes is driving their popularity among engineers and technicians.

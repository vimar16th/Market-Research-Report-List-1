<p><ul><li>Hydraulic-Mechanical Transmission</li><li>Hydraulic Transmission</li><li>Power Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-propelled belt loader market can be categorized into three types: hydraulic-mechanical transmission, hydraulic transmission, and power transmission. The hydraulic-mechanical transmission type utilizes a combination of hydraulic and mechanical components to transfer power from the engine to the belt loader, ensuring smooth operation. Hydraulic transmission type relies solely on hydraulic power to drive the belt loader, offering excellent control and maneuverability. Power transmission type employs alternative power sources like electric motors or internal combustion engines, providing flexibility and energy efficiency. These different types cater to varying customer preferences and requirements in the belt loader market.</p></p>

<p><p>The Chip-on-board (CoB) LED market comprises three main types: lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chip refers to the LED chip that is placed horizontally on the substrate, providing a wide light emission angle. Vertical chip refers to an LED chip that is positioned vertically, allowing for better heat dissipation and higher light output. Flip chip involves directly connecting the LED chip to the substrate, providing improved thermal management and enhanced electrical performance. These different types cater to varying applications and requirements within the LED market.</p></p>

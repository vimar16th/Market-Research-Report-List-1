<p><p>The Explosive Emulsifier market is categorized into two types: Polyisobutenyl Succinic Anhydride (PIBSA) Based and Span 80. PIBSA Based emulsifiers are derived from polyisobutene and succinic anhydride, bringing excellent stability and emulsifying properties to explosives. On the other hand, Span 80 is a type of sorbitan ester surfactant widely used as an emulsifier in the explosive industry. It helps stabilize the mixture of oil and water-based explosives, preventing separation and ensuring uniformity during storage and application. Both emulsifier types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and safety of explosive products.</p></p>

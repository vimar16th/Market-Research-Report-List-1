<p><ul><li>Cold Chain Logistics</li><li>Non-cold Chain Logistics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-Pharmaceutical Logistics market is divided into two types: Cold Chain Logistics and Non-cold Chain Logistics. Cold Chain Logistics refers to the transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive pharmaceutical products that require specific temperature control to ensure their efficacy and safety. This includes vaccines, serums, and other biopharmaceuticals that need to be kept refrigerated or frozen. On the other hand, Non-cold Chain Logistics primarily involves the transportation and storage of pharmaceutical products that do not require temperature control. This includes tablets, capsules, and other non-sensitive medications.</p></p>

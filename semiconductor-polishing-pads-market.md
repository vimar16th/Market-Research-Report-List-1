<p><p>The semiconductor polishing pads market consists of two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are made of rigid material and are ideal for removing large amounts of material during the polishing process. They provide high abrasive efficiency and are commonly used for coarse polishing applications. On the other hand, soft CMP pads are made of a softer material that provides gentler polishing action. They are used for fine polishing applications that require precise material removal and surface finishing.</p></p>

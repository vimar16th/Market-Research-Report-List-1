<p><p>Barrel-aged Port market encompasses different types, such as Tawny Port, Colheita Port, and Garrafeira Port. Tawny Port undergoes extended barrel aging, resulting in a golden-brown color and flavors of caramel and nuts. Colheita Port refers to a single vintage Port aged in barrels for at least seven years, offering a smoother taste profile. Finally, Garrafeira Port represents a blend of vintage and non-vintage Ports, aged in barrels before being transferred to glass demijohns for further aging. These variations offer unique characteristics, making the barrel-aged Port market diverse and appealing to different preferences.</p></p>

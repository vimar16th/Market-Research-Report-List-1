<p><p>Mechanical grates are used in various industrial applications to aid in the movement and separation of materials. There are two main types of mechanical grates: scroll type and reciprocating type. Scroll type mechanical grates operate by using a continuous scrolling motion to move materials along the grate. On the other hand, reciprocating type mechanical grates move materials back and forth on the grate through a reciprocating motion. Both types of grates serve the purpose of facilitating material movement and separation in different industrial processes.</p></p>

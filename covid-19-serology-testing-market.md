<p><p>Covid-19 Serology Testing Market includes various types of tests. Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT) is a quick and point-of-care test that detects antibodies in blood samples. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a laboratory-based test that determines the presence and levels of Covid-19 antibodies. Neutralization Assay evaluates the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus by testing their efficacy in preventing viral infection. All these testing methods play a crucial role in determining past exposure, immunity, and monitoring the spread of Covid-19 in the population.</p></p>

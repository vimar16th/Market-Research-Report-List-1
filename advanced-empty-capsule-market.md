<p><p>Advanced empty capsules are a type of pharmaceutical device used to enclose drug substances in a solid dosage form. These capsules provide a convenient and effective way to administer drugs as they offer protection, stability, and targeted delivery to specific sites in the body. Advanced empty capsules are made from various materials such as gelatin, vegetable cellulose, and starch.</p><p>The current outlook of the advanced empty capsule market is promising. The market is driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, growing geriatric population, and a rising focus on personalized medicine. The demand for advanced empty capsules is also driven by the need for precise drug delivery systems and the growing popularity of self-medication.</p><p>Furthermore, the advancements in capsule manufacturing technologies, such as the development of vegetarian and enteric-coated capsules, are expected to fuel market growth. Vegetarian capsules are gaining popularity among health-conscious consumers, while enteric-coated capsules provide protection to the drug from gastric acid in the stomach, ensuring intact delivery to the intestine.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has further increased the demand for advanced empty capsules, as there has been a surge in the production of vaccines, antiviral drugs, and supplements. This has led to a significant boost in the market growth.</p><p>Looking into the future, the advanced empty capsule market is expected to continue its growth trajectory.

<p><p>The Membrane Pleated Filter market includes four popular types of filters: PP Filter, PES Filter, PTFE Filter, and Nylon Filter. PP filters are made of polypropylene and are known for their excellent chemical resistance. PES filters are constructed from polyethersulfone and are commonly used in pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries due to their high flow rates and low protein binding properties. PTFE filters, made of polytetrafluoroethylene, offer superior chemical resistance and are often used in aggressive chemical environments. Nylon filters are made of nylon polymers and are suitable for a wide range of applications due to their compatibility with various solvents and strong mechanical strength.</p></p>

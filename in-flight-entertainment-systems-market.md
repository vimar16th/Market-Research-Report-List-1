The introduction of wireless IFE systems has further expanded the market reach, as it allows passengers to connect their own devices without the need for built-in screens.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in connectivity solutions like satellite-based Wi-Fi have revolutionized the in-flight entertainment experience. Passengers can now access high-speed internet and stream content directly to their devices. However, as the industry recovers and air travel resumes, the demand for IFE systems is expected to bounce back. The need for cleanliness and hygiene standards might lead to an increased adoption of touchless or voice-controlled interfaces in IFE systems.</p><p>Overall, the future of the in-flight entertainment systems market seems promising, with technological advancements and rising passenger demand driving the growth in the industry.</p></p>

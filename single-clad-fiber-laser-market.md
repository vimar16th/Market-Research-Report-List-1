A Continuous Wave Fiber Laser emits a continuous, steady laser beam, making it ideal for applications requiring a high power output. On the other hand, a Pulsed Fiber Laser emits laser beams in short pulses. This type is suitable for applications needing high peak power or precise control over energy delivery. Both types have their unique advantages and find applications in various industries like manufacturing, aerospace, and medical.</p></p>

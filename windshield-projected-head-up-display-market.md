These vehicles require advanced display systems to present crucial information related to the vehicle's performance, surroundings, and navigation. These advanced displays can overlay real-time information onto the real-world view, enhancing the driving experience by providing useful information on the road conditions, potential hazards, and even virtual navigation markers.</p><p>Considering these factors, it is projected that the windshield projected head-up display market will witness a steady growth rate of 11% during the forecasted period.

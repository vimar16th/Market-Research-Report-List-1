<p><p>Industrial automation and control is a field that involves the use of various technological systems and devices to automate and control industrial processes. These systems include Computer Numerical Control (CNC) routers, which control machine movements through pre-programmed instructions. Machine vision systems use cameras and image processing algorithms to inspect and analyze products. Manufacturing execution systems (MES) monitor and control production activities in real-time. Plant asset management optimizes the performance and maintenance of industrial assets. Product lifecycle management (PLM) manages the entire lifecycle of a product. Programmable logic control systems (PLC) automate and control machinery and processes. Supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) systems monitor and control industrial processes. Other systems may include robotic systems, control panels, and motion control systems.</p></p>

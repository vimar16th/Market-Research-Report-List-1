<p><p>The Molasses Extract Market offers different types of molasses: Fancy Molasses, Blackstrap Molasses, and Others. Fancy molasses is a light, sweet form obtained during the first boiling of sugarcane juice. It has a mild flavor and is commonly used in baking and cooking. Blackstrap molasses is the darkest and thickest variant, obtained from the third boiling. It has a robust, bitter taste and is often used for its nutritional value. Lastly, the Others category includes unique molasses varieties such as sorghum molasses or pomegranate molasses, providing distinct flavors for various culinary applications.</p></p>

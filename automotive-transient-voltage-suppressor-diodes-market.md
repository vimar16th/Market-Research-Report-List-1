<p><p>The Automotive Transient Voltage Suppressor Diodes market consists of two main types: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against voltage transients in one direction only, commonly used in applications such as automotive electronics. On the other hand, Bi-polar TVS diodes provide protection against voltage transients in both directions, making them suitable for applications where the polarity of voltage transients can change, such as automotive systems with alternators. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding automotive electronics by diverting excessive voltage transients away from sensitive components.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Ground-Based Radome Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shell Structure</li><li>Spherical Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ground-Based Radome Market includes two main types of structures: Shell Structure and Spherical Structure. Shell Structure radomes are designed with a smooth, curved shape that resembles an elongated vault. They offer high structural rigidity and aerodynamic efficiency, making them suitable for various applications. On the other hand, Spherical Structure radomes have a round shape, resembling a dome. They provide enhanced protection against wind loads and are often preferred for radars and satellite communication systems due to their ability to distribute stress evenly. Both types of radomes play crucial roles in protecting ground-based equipment and ensuring reliable signal transmission.</p></p>

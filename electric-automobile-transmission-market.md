<p><p>The electric automobile transmission market consists of two main types: single-gear transmission and multi-gear transmission. Single-gear transmission refers to an electric vehicle that operates with only one fixed gear ratio. This type is simpler, lighter, and more energy-efficient, making it suitable for smaller electric cars or those focusing on urban driving. On the other hand, multi-gear transmission includes electric vehicles that have multiple gear ratios. This type allows for better acceleration, performance, and higher top speeds, making it more suitable for larger electric cars or those designed for long-distance driving.</p></p>

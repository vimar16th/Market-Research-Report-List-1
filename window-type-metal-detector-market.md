<p><p>Window-type metal detectors are used in various industries to detect metallic contaminants in products or materials passing through a conveyor belt. There are two main types in the market: balanced coil systems and ferrous-in-foil systems. Balanced coil systems use two separate coils that generate opposing magnetic fields to detect metal contaminants. Ferrous-in-foil systems rely on specially designed coils to detect iron or steel particles within aluminum or brass foil packaging. Both types are efficient in detecting metallic impurities and ensuring product quality and safety.</p></p>

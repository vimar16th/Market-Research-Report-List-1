<p><p>Packaging Print Inks Market: </p><p>Lithographic Printing: This market involves the use of lithographic printing inks for packaging materials. This method uses a printing plate to transfer ink onto a rubber blanket, which then prints onto the packaging material.</p><p>Gravure Printing: This market utilizes gravure printing inks for packaging. Gravure printing employs a cylinder with engraved cells that pick up and transfer ink onto the packaging material.</p><p>Flexographic Printing: This market involves the use of flexographic printing inks for packaging. This method uses flexible rubber plates to transfer ink onto the packaging material.</p><p>Digital Printing: This market involves the use of digital printing inks for packaging. Digital printing technology enables direct printing onto packaging materials, bypassing the need for printing plates or cylinders.</p></p>

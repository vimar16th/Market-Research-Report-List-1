<p><p>The hardwood floor cleaner market can be categorized into three types: water cleaner, semi-aqueous cleaner, and non-aqueous cleaner. Water cleaners are designed to be dissolved in water and used for regular floor cleaning. Semi-aqueous cleaners are a combination of water and other solvents, providing a stronger cleaning solution for tougher stains and dirt. Non-aqueous cleaners, on the other hand, are oil-based or solvent-based, offering powerful cleaning agents that penetrate and remove deep-seated dirt and grime. Each type caters to different cleaning needs and offers varying levels of effectiveness.</p></p>

<p><p>PU coating, also known as polyurethane coating, is a type of protective liquid or powder coating applied to various surfaces. In terms of market types, there are four main variations: solvent-borne, water-borne, high solids, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings contain organic solvents as a base, while water-borne coatings use water as a base. High solids coatings have a high solids content and low volatile organic compounds, making them environmentally friendly. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and then cured through heat to form a hard, protective coating.</p></p>

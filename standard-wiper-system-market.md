<p><p>The Standard Wiper System market comprises three major types: Wiper Motor, Windshield Wiper, and Rain Sensor. Wiper Motor is responsible for controlling the movement of the windshield wipers, ensuring effective cleaning of the windshield. Windshield Wipers are the mechanical arms that hold the wiper blades and swipe across the windshield to remove dirt and debris. Rain Sensors detect rainfall and automatically activate the wiper system, providing a convenient and safe solution for drivers. These three components work together to enhance visibility and maintain a clear windshield for optimal driving conditions.</p></p>

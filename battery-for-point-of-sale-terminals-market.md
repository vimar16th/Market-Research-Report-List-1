<p><p>The battery market for Point of Sale (POS) terminals can be classified into three main types: Lead-Acid Batteries, Li-Ion Batteries, and Ni-Cd Batteries. Lead-Acid Batteries are commonly used due to their low cost and reliability, but they are heavy and have limited capacity. Li-Ion Batteries are lightweight, offer better capacity, and have a longer lifespan, making them a popular choice. Ni-Cd Batteries, although less common, provide a high charge and discharge rate, making them suitable for some specialized applications. These different battery types cater to the diverse needs of the POS terminal market.</p></p>

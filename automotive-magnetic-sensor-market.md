<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Automotive Magnetic Sensor market comprises three types: AMR, GMR, and TMR. AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance Effect) sensors utilize a special material to measure the change in resistance caused by a magnetic field. GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance Effect) sensors detect magnetic fields through variations in resistance in a multi-layered structure. TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance Effect) sensors, on the other hand, use quantum tunneling to measure magnetic fields. These sensors play a crucial role in automotive applications by accurately detecting and measuring magnetic fields for various purposes.</p></p>

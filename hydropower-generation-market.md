<p><p>Hydropower generation market can be categorized into two types: Large Hydropower and Small Hydropower. Large Hydropower refers to the generation of electricity from water flow through large dams, typically with capacities of more than 10 MW. It involves harnessing the power of large water bodies like rivers and lakes. On the other hand, Small Hydropower involves generating electricity from smaller water bodies or streams with capacities usually below 10 MW. Small Hydropower plants are often built on smaller rivers, canals, and even man-made diversions.</p></p>

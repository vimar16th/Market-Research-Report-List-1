<p><strong>The Aluminum Foil Fiberglass Cloth Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Aluminum Foil Fiberglass Cloth</li><li>Double-sided Aluminum Foil Fiberglass Cloth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum foil fiberglass cloth market consists of two types: single-sided aluminum foil fiberglass cloth and double-sided aluminum foil fiberglass cloth. Single-sided aluminum foil fiberglass cloth has one side covered with aluminum foil, providing excellent heat insulation and moisture resistance properties. It is commonly used for thermal insulation, smoke and fire curtains, and radiant heat reflection applications. On the other hand, double-sided aluminum foil fiberglass cloth has both sides covered with aluminum foil, offering enhanced heat insulation and barrier properties. It finds applications in HVAC duct insulation, pipe insulation, and aerospace industries.</p></p>

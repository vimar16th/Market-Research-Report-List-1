<p><p>The waterproof tarpaulins market is divided into two main types: PVC tarpaulin and PE tarpaulin. PVC tarpaulins are made from polyvinyl chloride material, which makes them highly durable, resilient, and resistant to UV rays. They are commonly used for heavy-duty applications such as construction covers and truck curtains. On the other hand, PE tarpaulins are made from polyethylene material, offering a more economical option. PE tarpaulins are lightweight, flexible, and mainly used for temporary purposes like camping, agriculture, and general household protection.</p></p>

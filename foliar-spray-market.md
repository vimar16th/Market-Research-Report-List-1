<p><p>Foliar spray market types include nitrogenous, phosphatic, potassic, and micronutrients. Nitrogenous foliar sprays provide plants with nitrogen, an essential nutrient for growth and development. Phosphatic sprays contain phosphorus, which promotes root development and flower formation. Potassic sprays are rich in potassium, which helps plants resist diseases and improve fruit quality. Micronutrient sprays contain trace elements such as iron, zinc, and manganese, which are necessary for enzymatic processes and overall plant health. These different types of sprays are used to address specific nutrient deficiencies in plants and enhance their overall productivity.</p></p>

<p><p>The Liquid Phenoxy Resins market consists of two types: Waterborne Phenoxy Resins and Solventborne Phenoxy Resins. Waterborne Phenoxy Resins are formulated using water as a carrier, making them environmentally friendly and easy to work with. They are primarily used in applications where low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions are required. On the other hand, Solventborne Phenoxy Resins are formulated with a solvent as a carrier, offering excellent adhesion and corrosion resistance properties. They find applications in coatings, adhesives, and composites, where durability is essential.</p></p>

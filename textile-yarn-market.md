<p><p>Textile yarn refers to a continuous twisted strand of fibers used for weaving, knitting, and making fabrics. This trend is driven by the demand for sustainable fashion and eco-friendly products, leading to the adoption of yarns made from organic cotton, bamboo, hemp, and recycled polyester.</p><p>Another trend is the rise in demand for specialized yarns with improved properties. This includes high-performance yarns for sports apparel, medical textiles, and technical textiles used in industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction. These specialized yarns offer enhanced strength, durability, moisture-wicking, temperature regulation, and antimicrobial properties.

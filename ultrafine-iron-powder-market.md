<p><p>The ultrafine iron powder market consists of two main types: carbonyl iron powder and electrolytic iron powder. Carbonyl iron powder is produced through the decomposition of iron pentacarbonyl gas, resulting in particles with a high purity level and a spherical shape. It is commonly used in the production of diamond tools, electromagnetic devices, and magnetic materials. On the other hand, electrolytic iron powder is manufactured through an electrolysis process, producing irregularly-shaped particles. It finds applications in automotive components, chemical catalysts, and electrical appliances.</p></p>

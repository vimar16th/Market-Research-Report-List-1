<p><p>AC electric motors are widely used in the oil and gas industry. There are two main types: induction motors and synchronous motors. Induction motors are commonly used for pumping and compression applications due to their simplicity, ruggedness, and cost-effectiveness. They rely on the magnetic field induction to generate torque. Synchronous motors, on the other hand, provide a constant speed and high starting torque, making them suitable for applications that require precise control and high performance. Both motor types play crucial roles in the oil and gas market, powering various equipment and machinery.</p></p>

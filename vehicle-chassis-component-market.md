Track rollers/load rollers are used to support and guide the track system, while crawler chains are responsible for transferring power from the vehicle's engine to the track system. Idler and sprocket help to maintain the tension and drive the track system, and track shoes/rubber tracks provide traction and stability. Other components like bushings and seals are essential for smooth movement and preventing leaks in the system.</p></p>

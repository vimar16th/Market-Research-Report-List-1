<p><p>The transparent bio-based barrier film market consists of two main types: monolayer and multilayer films. Monolayer films are made of a single layer of bio-based material, providing basic protection against oxygen, moisture, and other external factors. On the other hand, multilayer films are composed of multiple layers, each with its own unique properties, offering enhanced barrier protection and tailored functionalities. These films are used in various industries such as food packaging and agriculture, where preserving product quality and extending shelf life are crucial requirements.</p></p>

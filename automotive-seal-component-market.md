<p><p>The automotive seal component market offers three main types: EPDM, NR, and SBR. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is known for its exceptional resistance to weathering, aging, and heat, making it ideal for vehicles exposed to extreme conditions. NR (Natural Rubber) is a flexible and resilient material, commonly used in seals due to its excellent tear resistance and high tensile strength. SBR (Styrene Butadiene Rubber) is highly durable and suitable for various applications in the automotive industry due to its excellent abrasion resistance and low heat build-up.</p></p>

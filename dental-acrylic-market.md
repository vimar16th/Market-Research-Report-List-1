<p><p>The Dental Acrylic Market comprises different types of acrylic resins used in dentistry. Heat Cure Acrylic Resin is cured by heat, providing a stronger and more durable final product. Self-Curing Acrylic Resin does not require external heat for curing and is commonly used for denture repairs. Cold Cure Acrylic Resin is cured at room temperature, making it ideal for on-site repairs. Lastly, Light-Cured Acrylic Resin is polymerized using a dental curing light, offering a quick and precise curing process.</p></p>

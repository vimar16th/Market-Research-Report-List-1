<p><strong>The Rosin Based Pastes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Activity Level</li><li>Moderate Activity Level</li><li>High Activity Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rosin-based pastes are classified into three market types based on their activity levels: low, moderate, and high. Low activity level rosin pastes are formulated with low amounts of active rosin, making them suitable for applications that require minimal reactivity or tackiness. Moderate activity level pastes contain a moderate concentration of active rosin, providing balanced adhesion and reactivity for a wide range of applications. High activity level rosin pastes, on the other hand, have a higher concentration of active rosin, making them ideal for demanding applications that require strong adhesion and fast reactivity.</p></p>

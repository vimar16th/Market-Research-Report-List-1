<p><p>The aerospace semi-finished steel materials market can be categorized into four types: rods, sheets, plates, and tubes. Rods are cylindrical bars made of steel and are typically used for structural support or fastening purposes. Sheets refer to thin, flat pieces of steel that are often used in the fabrication of aircraft components. Plates are thicker and larger sheets that are employed in the construction of critical sections of an aircraft. Tubes are hollow cylindrical structures made of steel and are utilized in various parts of the aircraft, including fuel systems and hydraulic systems.</p></p>

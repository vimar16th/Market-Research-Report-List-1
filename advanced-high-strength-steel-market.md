<p><p>The Advanced High-strength Steel (AHSS) market is categorized into different types based on its properties and composition. Dual Phase (DP) steel possesses a high strength and good formability combination, making it suitable for structural applications. Transformation-induced Plasticity (TRIP) steel exhibits exceptional energy absorption abilities due to a combination of strain hardening and transformation-induced plasticity. Complex Phase (CP) steel provides high strength and excellent formability through a multiphase microstructure. Other types of AHSS in the market include Martensitic steel, Twinning Induced Plasticity (TWIP) steel, and others, each offering unique characteristics to cater to specific requirements.</p></p>

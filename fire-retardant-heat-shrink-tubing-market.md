<p><p>The fire retardant heat shrink tubing market consists of two main types: single wall tubing and dual wall tubing. Single wall tubing is a thin layer of heat shrink material that provides insulation, protection, and resistance to fire. It is commonly used in applications that require light-weight and space-saving solutions. On the other hand, dual wall tubing consists of an outer layer of heat shrink material and an inner layer of adhesive. This type offers higher protection and sealing capabilities, making it suitable for more demanding applications that require superior insulation and moisture resistance.</p></p>

<p><p>A Simultaneous Thermal Analyzer (STA) is an instrument that combines the capabilities of both a thermogravimetric analyzer (TGA) and a differential scanning calorimeter (DSC) into one unit. It is used for simultaneous measurement of the weight change and heat flow of a sample as it is subjected to controlled heating or cooling.</p><p>The STA market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for STA instruments in various industries such as polymers, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals for research and development activities is one of the major factors driving the market growth.

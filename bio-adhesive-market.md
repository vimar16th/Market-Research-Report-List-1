<p><p>The bio adhesive market encompasses two main types: plant-based adhesive and animal-based adhesive. Plant-based adhesives are derived from natural compounds found in plants, such as starch, cellulose, and resins. These adhesives are eco-friendly and commonly used in applications like paper packaging and woodworking. On the other hand, animal-based adhesives are derived from animal proteins like collagen and gelatin. These adhesives are known for their high adhesive strength and are commonly used in industries like medical, textiles, and bookbinding. Both types of bio adhesives offer sustainable and versatile alternatives to traditional synthetic adhesives.</p></p>

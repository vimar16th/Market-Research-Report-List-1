They are suitable for basic dental procedures. Fully-adjustable articulators, on the other hand, offer a wide range of adjustments, allowing for precise replication of jaw movements. These articulators are commonly used in complex dental treatments, such as full mouth rehabilitation and occlusion adjustments.</p></p>

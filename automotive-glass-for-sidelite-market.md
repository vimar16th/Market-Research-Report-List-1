<p><ul><li>Tempered</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive glass for sidelite market consists of three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is produced by heating and cooling the material rapidly, making it stronger and more resistant to breakage. Laminated glass, on the other hand, is made by sandwiching a layer of plastic between two layers of glass, providing enhanced safety as it remains intact even when shattered. The "others" category includes various types like coated glass, acoustic glass, and infrared reflective glass that offer additional functionalities such as noise reduction, improved insulation, and UV protection.</p></p>

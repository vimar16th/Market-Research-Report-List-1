<p><p>Bus Driving Recorder Market Types:</p><p>1. Integrated: Integrated bus driving recorders are built-in systems that are installed directly into the bus's dashboard or control panel. These recorders are seamlessly integrated into the bus's existing electrical system, allowing for real-time monitoring and recording of various data such as vehicle speed, route details, and driver behavior. Integrated recorders are typically more sophisticated and offer advanced features such as GPS tracking and remote data access.</p><p>2. Portable: Portable bus driving recorders are standalone devices that can be easily installed and uninstalled in different buses as needed. These recorders are designed to be compact and portable, allowing for flexibility and convenience. Portable recorders often use external power sources such as batteries or vehicle cigarette lighters and are equipped with memory cards or onboard storage to save the recorded data. These recorders are suitable for companies with a fleet of buses or for temporary monitoring purposes.</p></p>

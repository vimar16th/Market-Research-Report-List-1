<p><p>The deployable non-military shelter market is categorized into two main types: soft wall and hard wall. Soft wall shelters are made of lightweight materials like fabric or PVC and are easy to assemble and transport. They provide quick temporary housing and are commonly used in emergency situations or for recreational purposes. On the other hand, hard wall shelters are made with rigid materials like fiberglass or steel and offer more durability and long-term functionality. They are suitable for more permanent applications such as construction sites or mining operations.</p></p>

<p><p>The polypropene market consists of two main types: homopolymer and copolymer. Homopolymer polypropene is made from a single type of propene monomer and has a high melting point, making it suitable for applications requiring high heat resistance. On the other hand, copolymer polypropene is created by blending propene monomers with other monomers, resulting in improved flexibility and impact resistance. Copolymers are often used in products like automotive parts and packaging materials due to their durability and versatility.</p></p>

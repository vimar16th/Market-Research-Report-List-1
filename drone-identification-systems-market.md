<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Drone identification systems consist of two main market types: detection and identification technologies, and mitigation technologies. Detection technologies are designed to identify the presence of drones in a specific area by utilizing sensors, radar systems, radio frequency scanners, or acoustic devices. Identification technologies take detection a step further by providing detailed information about the drone, such as its make, model, and operator, using methods like video analytics, machine learning, or electronic signatures. Mitigation technologies are solutions that are developed to counteract potential threats posed by drones, including jamming systems, net guns, or laser-based systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive rear windshield market is divided into two types: thermoset material and thermoplastic material. Thermoset materials are characterized by their ability to be molded into a specific shape and once set, cannot be reshaped. They offer excellent resistance to heat and chemicals, making them suitable for rear windshields. On the other hand, thermoplastic materials can be reshaped multiple times when heated and cooled. They provide high impact resistance and are commonly used in rear windshields due to their lightweight nature and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>

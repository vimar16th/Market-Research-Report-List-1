Composite hoses have layers of different materials, such as polypropylene and polyethylene, providing excellent flexibility and chemical resistance. Stainless Steel hoses are constructed with a stainless steel outer cover, offering higher durability and resistance to corrosion. These types of hoses are used in the aviation industry for safely transferring fuel from the refueling vehicle to the aircraft.</p></p>

Termite powder is a dry powder that is applied to termite nests, acting as a poison when consumed by the termites. Termite baits consist of treated materials that attract termites, which then carry the bait back to their nest, causing the entire colony to be eliminated. These products are used to effectively combat and prevent termite damage in homes and buildings.</p></p>

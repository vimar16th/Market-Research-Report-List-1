<p><p>The loud car horns market consists of three main types: air horns, electronic horns, and electromagnetic horns. Air horns utilize compressed air to produce a loud sound, typically used in large vehicles or emergency vehicles. Electronic horns, on the other hand, produce sound through an electrical system, commonly found in smaller vehicles. Electromagnetic horns generate sound by using an electromagnet to vibrate a diaphragm, creating a loud and distinct tone. Each type of horn caters to different vehicle sizes and purposes, offering various options for customers in the market.</p></p>

<p><p>The Burnt Dolomite market can be classified into two types; Dead Burnt Dolomite and Light Burnt Dolomite. Dead Burnt Dolomite is obtained by heating dolomite at very high temperatures, resulting in a product that is chemically inactive. It is used in the production of refractory bricks and in the steel industry. On the other hand, Light Burnt Dolomite is produced by heating dolomite at relatively lower temperatures, resulting in a product that retains certain chemical reactivity. It finds applications in agriculture, water treatment, and the construction industry.</p></p>

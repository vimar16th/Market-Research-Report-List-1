<p><p>Enterprise Key Management (EKM) solutions are categorized into two market types: On-Premise and Cloud. On-Premise EKM solutions are installed locally within an organization's infrastructure, allowing for full control over key management and data security. This type provides physical control but requires more maintenance and resources. In contrast, Cloud EKM solutions are hosted and managed by third-party providers on remote servers. They offer convenience and scalability, eliminating the need for on-site infrastructure and maintenance. However, they rely on trusting the cloud provider's security measures and can face potential data breach risks.</p></p>

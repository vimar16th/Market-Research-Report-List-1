<p><p>The craft beer brewery equipment market includes two main types: semi-automatic and full automatic. Semi-automatic equipment requires some manual intervention during the brewing process, such as adjusting temperature or flow rates. It offers brewers more control and customization options but may require more labor. On the other hand, full automatic equipment automates most brewing processes, significantly reducing manual intervention. It provides ease of use and higher production efficiency but may offer less flexibility for customization. Brewers can choose the type of equipment based on their preference for control, labor requirements, and production efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>Methyl Methacrylate (MMA) Monomer Market Types include the ACH Method, Isobutylene Method, and Ethylene Method. The ACH Method involves producing MMA through an acetone cyanohydrin process. The Isobutylene Method produces MMA by reacting isobutylene and formaldehyde. The Ethylene Method obtains MMA by reacting propylene and formaldehyde. These methods are used to create different types of MMA monomers, which are key ingredients in the production of plastics, resins, adhesives, and coatings. Each method has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>

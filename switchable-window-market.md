<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The switchable window market comprises three types of smart glass: electrochromic smart glass, SPD smart glass, and PDLC smart glass. Electrochromic smart glass controls light transmission by applying a low voltage current. SPD smart glass uses suspended particle technology to adjust its tint according to external conditions. PDLC smart glass relies on a polymer dispersed liquid crystal matrix to switch between transparent and opaque states. These smart glass types provide a range of options for controlling privacy, glare, and energy efficiency in various architectural and automotive applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Soda lime absorbent is a mixture commonly used in medical and laboratory settings to absorb carbon dioxide (CO2) gas. It is typically composed of a combination of calcium hydroxide (Ca(OH)2) and sodium hydroxide (NaOH) with varying amounts of water. When CO2 is passed through the soda lime absorbent, it reacts with the calcium hydroxide to form calcium carbonate (CaCO3), effectively removing the CO2 from the surrounding air or gas mixture.</p><p>The soda lime absorbent market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing adoption of soda lime absorbent in the healthcare industry for applications such as anesthesia and respiratory therapy. Additionally, the growing demand for carbon dioxide removal systems in industrial processes and laboratories is further propelling the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the soda lime absorbent market is the development of advanced compositions to enhance the efficiency and lifespan of the absorbent. Manufacturers are focusing on improving the formulation of soda lime absorbent to increase its CO2 absorption capacity and minimize the production of harmful by-products. This is important for maintaining patient safety during medical procedures and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is also witnessing a surge in demand for disposable soda lime absorbent products, as they offer convenience and reduce the risk of cross-contamination. This trend is especially prominent in hospitals and clinics where infection control is crucial.</p><p>Overall, with the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, rising healthcare spending, and growing industrial applications, the soda lime absorbent market is expected to experience substantial growth at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period.</p></p>

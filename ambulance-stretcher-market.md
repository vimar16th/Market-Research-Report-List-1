As governments invest in improving emergency medical services, the demand for ambulance stretchers is expected to witness significant growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the ambulance stretcher market is poised for positive growth in the coming years, driven by factors such as increasing emergency cases, a growing elderly population, technological advancements, and government investments in healthcare infrastructure.

<p><p>Side thrusters are used to provide additional maneuverability to ships and vessels. There are three main market types for side thrusters. Tunnel side thrusters are installed within the vessel's hull, which helps in improving the vessel's hydrodynamics. Externally mounted side thrusters are attached to the vessel's exterior, providing flexibility in installation and maintenance. Waterjet side thrusters use waterjets to generate thrust, offering high maneuverability and efficient propulsion. These different market types cater to the diverse needs and requirements of ships and vessels in terms of maneuverability and performance.</p></p>

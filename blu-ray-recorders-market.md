<p><p>Blu-ray recorders are available in two main market types: internally installed and externally installed. Internally installed recorders are built into a device, such as a desktop computer or a laptop, during the manufacturing process. They are already integrated into the system and cannot be easily removed or replaced. On the other hand, externally installed recorders are separate devices that can be connected to a computer or other compatible devices via USB or other interfaces. They are portable and can be easily attached or detached from the system as needed.</p></p>

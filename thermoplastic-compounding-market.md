<p><p>The thermoplastic compounding market can be divided into three main types: thermoplastic polymers, thermosetting polymers, and thermoplastic elastomers. Thermoplastic polymers are materials that can be melted and reshaped multiple times without losing their properties. Thermosetting polymers, on the other hand, undergo a permanent chemical change when heated, becoming rigid and resistant to melting. Thermoplastic elastomers combine the properties of rubber and plastic, showing both elasticity and the ability to be melted and reprocessed. These different types cater to various industries and applications within the thermoplastic compounding market.</p></p>

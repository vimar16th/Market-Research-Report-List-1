<p><p>Autogyros, also known as gyroplanes or gyrocopters, are aircraft that rely on unpowered free-spinning rotor blades to generate lift and an engine-driven propeller for propulsion. They combine the vertical takeoff and landing capability of a helicopter with the forward flight characteristics of an airplane. Autogyros offer several advantages such as shorter takeoff and landing distances, lower operating costs, and simpler mechanical systems compared to traditional aircraft.</p><p>The autogyros market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. The increasing demand for cost-effective and versatile aircraft for various applications such as surveillance, aerial photography, tourism, and recreational purposes is driving the market growth.

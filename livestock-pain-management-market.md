<p><p>Livestock pain management market includes two types: external use and internal use. External use products are applied on the animal's outer body surface and include sprays, creams, or gels to alleviate pain. They are commonly used for injuries, wounds, or inflammation relief. On the other hand, internal use products are administered orally or injected into the livestock to manage pain from within. These products may include medications, analgesics, or anesthetics, which target the animal's internal systems to provide pain relief. Both types contribute to the overall pain management for livestock.</p></p>

<p><p>Femtosecond lasers are advanced laser systems capable of emitting ultra-short pulses in the femtosecond range. Titanium-sapphire lasers use a crystal as the gain medium and offer a broad wavelength range, making them versatile and suitable for various applications. Diode-pumped lasers utilize diodes to pump the gain medium, enhancing efficiency and stability. Fiber lasers employ an optical fiber as the gain medium, resulting in compact and robust systems. Mode-locked diode lasers generate ultra-short pulses using semiconductor diode lasers, offering cost-effective and compact solutions for femtosecond laser applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The medical transseptal access system market is divided into two types: integrated transseptal devices and non-integrated transseptal devices. Integrated transseptal devices are designed to provide a complete solution for transseptal access procedures, including sheaths, dilators, and guidewires all within a single device. Non-integrated transseptal devices, on the other hand, require separate components for transseptal access procedures. Both types of devices serve the purpose of safe and effective access to the left atrium during cardiac interventions, but they differ in their design approach and product offerings.</p></p>

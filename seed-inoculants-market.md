<p><p>Seed inoculants market refers to the industry that produces products used to treat seeds with beneficial microorganisms. There are three main types of seed inoculants: bacterials, fungal inoculants, and composite inoculants. Bacterials contain bacteria that form symbiotic relationships with plants, fixing atmospheric nitrogen. Fungal inoculants contain beneficial fungi that enhance plant nutrient uptake and protect against pathogens. Composite inoculants combine both bacteria and fungi to provide dual benefits. These seed inoculants help improve plant growth, yield, and overall plant health, leading to increased agricultural productivity.</p></p>

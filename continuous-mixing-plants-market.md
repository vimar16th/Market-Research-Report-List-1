<p><p>Continuous mixing plants are a type of asphalt mixing plant that allow for the continuous production of asphalt mixture. These plants have the ability to mix asphalt continuously, which makes them popular in large-scale construction projects like highways, airports, and urban roads.</p><p>Continuous mixing plants consist of several components including a cold feed system, a continuous drum mixer, and a hot storage silo. The cold feed system controls the flow of aggregate materials into the drum mixer, where they are mixed with hot asphalt binder. The mixture is then stored in the hot storage silo until it is ready to be loaded onto trucks for transportation.</p><p>The continuous mixing plants market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for asphalt in construction projects, especially in developing countries, is the key factor driving the market growth.

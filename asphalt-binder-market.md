<p><p>Asphalt binder is a crucial component used in road construction. The market for asphalt binder is classified into two main types: modified asphalt and impregnated asphalt. Modified asphalt involves the addition of polymers or chemicals to the binder, enhancing its qualities such as resistance to cracking and durability. On the other hand, impregnated asphalt involves the impregnation of aggregates with the binder, increasing its strength and stability. Both types of asphalt binder are used to improve the performance and lifespan of road surfaces, ensuring safer and more reliable transportation networks.</p></p>

<p><p>The Explosion-proof Fan Market is divided into two types: Explosion-Proof Centrifugal Fan and Explosion-Proof Axial Flow Fan. Explosion-Proof Centrifugal Fans are designed to handle high-pressure applications and are generally used in industries where hazardous gases or materials are present. They have a central hub and multiple blades that rotate to create airflow. On the other hand, Explosion-Proof Axial Flow Fans are ideal for low-pressure applications and are commonly used in ventilation systems. They have a series of blades that rotate around a central axis, creating a linear airflow. Both types are specifically designed to operate safely in hazardous environments.</p></p>

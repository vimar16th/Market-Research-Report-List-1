<p><p>Photoresist ancillaries market includes various types such as anti-reflective coatings, photoresist developers, edge bead removers, and other types. Anti-reflective coatings are used to reduce unwanted reflections on the surface of a photoresist. Photoresist developers are used to selectively remove the exposed or unexposed areas of photoresist. Edge bead removers are used to eliminate excess photoresist found at the edges of a substrate. Other types may include primers, adhesion promoters, and specialty chemicals used in the photoresist process. These ancillaries are essential in enhancing the performance and efficiency of photoresist materials.</p></p>

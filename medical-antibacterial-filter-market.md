<p><p>The medical antibacterial filter market consists of two main types: electrostatic filters and mechanical filters. Electrostatic filters use an electric charge to attract and trap bacteria and other particulate matter, providing effective filtration. On the other hand, mechanical filters physically block and capture bacteria and other contaminants through a barrier or sieve-like mechanism. Both types of filters play a crucial role in preventing the spread of infection in medical settings, but they differ in their filtration mechanisms.</p></p>

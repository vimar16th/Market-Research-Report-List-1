<p><p>Dispersing agents are substances used to improve the dispersion of solids in liquid mediums. There are different types of dispersing agents in the market. Anionic dispersing agents have a negatively charged surface and are commonly used in industries such as paints, coatings, and dyes. Cationic dispersing agents, on the other hand, have a positively charged surface and are mainly utilized in applications like water treatment and textile auxiliaries. Lastly, the "others" category includes dispersing agents with different functionalities, such as non-ionic dispersing agents that do not have a charged surface but are still effective in promoting dispersion.</p></p>

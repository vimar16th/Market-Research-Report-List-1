<p><p>The advanced car stereo market consists of two main types: OEM and Aftermarket market. The OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) market refers to car stereos that are installed by the manufacturer during the production process. These stereos are specifically designed to integrate seamlessly with the vehicle's electronics and are typically included in new cars. On the other hand, the Aftermarket market refers to car stereos that are purchased separately and installed after the vehicle's purchase. These stereos offer a wide range of options and features, allowing car owners to customize their audio experience.</p></p>

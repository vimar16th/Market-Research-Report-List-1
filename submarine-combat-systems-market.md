SSK submarines are designed primarily for anti-submarine warfare and are equipped with torpedoes and underwater weapons for hunting and disabling enemy submarines. SSN submarines are nuclear-powered and are used for various purposes including reconnaissance, surveillance, and attacking surface ships and submarines. SSBN submarines are nuclear-powered as well and are specially designed for carrying and launching ballistic missiles, serving as strategic deterrents.</p></p>

<p><p>The digital switch-mode power supplies (SMPS) market is divided into two main types: AC/DC and DC/DC. AC/DC power supplies convert alternating current (AC) from a power source, like an electrical outlet, into direct current (DC) that can be used by electronic devices. These power supplies are commonly found in home appliances and electronic devices. On the other hand, DC/DC power supplies convert DC voltage from one level to another, making them suitable for applications such as battery-powered devices and automotive electronics. Both types have different functionalities and applications within the broader SMPS market.</p></p>

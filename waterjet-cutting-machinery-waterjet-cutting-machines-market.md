<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterjet cutting machinery includes pressurized water and abrasive mixture market types. Pressurized water cutting machines use high-pressure water streams to precisely cut a wide range of materials, such as foam, rubber, and thin metals. This method is suitable for industries that require high precision and minimal material waste. On the other hand, abrasive mixture cutting machines employ waterjets mixed with abrasive materials like garnet to cut thicker materials like concrete, stone, and metals. They offer increased cutting speed and versatility for industries that require heavy-duty cutting.</p></p>

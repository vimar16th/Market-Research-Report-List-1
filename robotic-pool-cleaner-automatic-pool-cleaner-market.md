<p><p>In the robotic pool cleaner market, there are two commonly observed types: Crawler Drive and Wheel Drive. Crawler Drive robotic pool cleaners have tracks similar to tanks and move around the pool with the help of these tracks. They offer excellent maneuverability and can efficiently clean irregular pool shapes. On the other hand, Wheel Drive robotic pool cleaners utilize wheels for movement. They excel in cleaning large, open areas and have a faster speed compared to Crawler Drive models. Both types are popular choices depending on the pool layout and cleaning requirements.</p></p>

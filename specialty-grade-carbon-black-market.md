<p><p>Specialty grade carbon black is a high-quality form of carbon black used in various industries. It can be categorized into different types based on its production process. Furnace black is produced through the combustion of hydrocarbons. Channel black is made by partially combusting natural gas. Thermal black is derived from natural gas and has a high structure, resulting in improved conductivity. Acetylene black is produced through the thermal decomposition of acetylene gas. Other types include lamp black and gas black, which have unique properties suitable for specific applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Surveying receivers are used in the field of land surveying to determine accurate positions and measurements. Handheld GNSS receivers are portable devices that allow surveyors to collect data anywhere, making them ideal for remote or challenging terrains. Fixed GNSS receivers, on the other hand, are permanently installed at specific locations for continuous monitoring and reference purposes.

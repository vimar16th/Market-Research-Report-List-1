<p><p>The automotive pressure switch is a device used in vehicles to monitor and control pressure levels in certain systems. The AC pressure switch, a type of automotive pressure switch, has different market types. The dual function pressure switch performs two functions: controlling the high-pressure cut-off (HPCO) and activating the radiator fan. The HPCO market refers specifically to switches that prevent the AC compressor from operating at excessively high pressures. The fan market includes switches that activate the radiator fan when the AC is turned on. Finally, the compressor cycling market involves switches that control the on/off cycling of the AC compressor to maintain desired pressure levels.</p></p>

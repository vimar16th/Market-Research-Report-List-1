<p><p>Sealant tape is available in different market types classified based on their density. Low-density sealant tape is less dense and is suitable for applications requiring minimal compression. Standard-density sealant tape is commonly used for general sealing purposes and provides moderate compression. Medium-density sealant tape offers higher compression and is ideal for applications that require a tighter seal. High-density sealant tape has the highest compression and is suitable for applications that demand a superior seal and resistance to movement or vibration. These different types cater to various sealing needs in different industries.</p></p>

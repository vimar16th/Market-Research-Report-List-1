<p><p>The Lithium Battery Manufacturing Machinery market can be categorized into three main types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post processing. Pretreatment machinery is responsible for the preparation of battery materials, such as coating and drying electrodes. Cell assembly machinery is used to assemble the battery cells, including processes like winding, stacking, and sealing. Post-processing machinery is involved in finalizing the batteries, such as formation and testing procedures. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall manufacturing process of lithium batteries.</p></p>

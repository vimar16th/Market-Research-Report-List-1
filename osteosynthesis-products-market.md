<p><strong>The Osteosynthesis Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Degradable Materials</li><li>Nondegradable Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The osteosynthesis products market is segregated into two types based on the materials used: degradable and nondegradable. Degradable materials, such as bioabsorbable implants, are designed to gradually break down and be absorbed by the body over time. These products eliminate the need for implant removal surgery. On the other hand, nondegradable materials, like metallic implants, are made to remain in the body permanently. They provide long-term stability but may require removal in some cases. The choice between degradable and nondegradable materials depends on factors like patient preferences, type of fracture, and post-operative requirements.</p></p>

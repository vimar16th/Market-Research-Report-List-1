<p><p>The ready mixed asphalt market consists of two main types: hot mix and cold mix. Hot mix asphalt is produced at high temperatures and is commonly used in road construction and maintenance projects. It offers enhanced durability and performance due to its higher quality and improved binding properties. On the other hand, cold mix asphalt is produced at lower temperatures and is mainly used for temporary repairs or during colder weather conditions. It provides a quick and cost-effective solution for minor road damages.</p></p>

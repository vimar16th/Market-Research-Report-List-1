<p><strong>The Polyurethane Timing Belt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trapezoidal Tooth Polyurethane Timing Belt</li><li>Arc Tooth Polyurethane Timing Belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane timing belts are categorized into two main types: trapezoidal tooth polyurethane timing belt and arc tooth polyurethane timing belt. The trapezoidal tooth polyurethane timing belt has trapezoidal-shaped teeth that engage with corresponding pulleys, providing reliable power transmission. On the other hand, the arc tooth polyurethane timing belt features curved teeth that allow for smoother engagement with pulleys, reducing noise and vibration. Both types of belts are widely used in various industries for their durability, precision, and efficient power transmission capabilities.</p></p>

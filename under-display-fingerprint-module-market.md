<p><strong>The Under-display Fingerprint Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The under-display fingerprint module market consists of three main types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic. Capacitive fingerprint modules use a small electrical charge to measure the ridges and valleys of a fingerprint, providing a high level of accuracy. Optical fingerprint modules utilize light to capture an image of the fingerprint, which is then analyzed for authentication. Ultrasonic fingerprint modules use ultrasonic waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, delivering enhanced security. These different module types provide various options for smartphone manufacturers to incorporate under-display fingerprint sensors.</p></p>

<p><p>The Feed Prebiotics Market includes three main types: Inulin, Fructooligosaccharide (FOS), and Isomaltooligosaccharide (IMO). Inulin is a soluble dietary fiber derived from plants, known for its ability to promote the growth of beneficial bacteria in the gut. FOS is a type of prebiotic that stimulates the growth of specific beneficial bacteria in the intestines. IMO, on the other hand, is a mixture of short-chain carbohydrates that also helps in promoting the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. These prebiotics are widely used in the animal feed industry to enhance gut health and overall performance of livestock.</p></p>

Pellets are small, cylindrical shaped pieces made from dried and ground alfalfa, often used in animal nutrition. The "others" category may include alfalfa sprouts, alfalfa extract, or alfalfa oil, which find applications in human diets, pharmaceuticals, or cosmetics, among other industries.</p></p>

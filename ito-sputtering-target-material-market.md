<p><p>ITO Sputtering Target Material market is categorized into two types: Plane Target and Rotating Target. Plane Target refers to a flat surface that is used in sputtering machines to deposit thin films onto a substrate. It is commonly used for large-scale production due to its high deposition rate. On the other hand, a Rotating Target involves a cylindrical shape that rotates during the sputtering process. This type is preferred for applications requiring uniform film thickness and is commonly used in research and development settings. Both types play a crucial role in the ITO Sputtering Target Material market.</p></p>

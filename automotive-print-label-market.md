<p><strong>The Automotive Print Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Glued Labels</li><li>Pressure Sensitive/Self Adhesive Labels</li><li>Liner-less labels</li><li>Multi-part Barcode Labels</li><li>In-mold labels</li><li>Shrink Sleeve Label</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive print label market encompasses various types of labels used in the automotive industry. Wet glued labels are applied using adhesive and water, while pressure sensitive/self-adhesive labels have an adhesive backing that sticks when pressure is applied. Liner-less labels do not have a liner, making them more eco-friendly. Multi-part barcode labels consist of multiple layers for efficient tracking. In-mold labels are permanently embedded in the product during the manufacturing process. Shrink sleeve labels are applied around a container and shrink to fit its shape, providing a 360-degree branding solution.</p></p>

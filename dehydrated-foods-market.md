<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dehydrated Foods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-Dried Foods</li><li>Freeze-Dried Foods</li><li>Vacuum-Dried Foods</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated foods market is categorized into different types based on the drying method employed. Spray-dried foods involve spraying the liquid food onto hot air, quickly evaporating the moisture content. Freeze-dried foods involve freezing the food and then removing the ice through sublimation, resulting in a dry product with minimal loss of nutrients and flavor. Vacuum-dried foods utilize a vacuum to remove moisture, maintaining the food's flavor and nutritional value. Other methods, such as drum drying, air drying, and sun drying, also exist, but are less commonly used.</p></p>

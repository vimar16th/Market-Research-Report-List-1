<p><p>The continuous extrusion machine market includes two main types: curvilinear type and radial type machines. Curvilinear type continuous extrusion machines are designed to produce long sections of curved or twisted profiles, commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction. On the other hand, radial type continuous extrusion machines are specifically designed to produce radial or circular profiles with high precision, ideal for applications in industries like electrical wiring, plumbing, and manufacturing. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, catering to specific profile requirements.</p></p>

Rigid plastic packing refers to packaging made from sturdy plastics, providing durability and protection for products. Soft plastic packing, on the other hand, involves flexible plastics that offer ease of handling and are commonly used for products like groceries. Lastly, paper packing involves using paper-based materials, such as cardboard or paperboard, for packaging, offering a more eco-friendly option. These packaging types cater to different product needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>The plastic pill bottle market is divided into two main types: Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). PET pill bottles are durable, transparent, and have good moisture resistance, making them suitable for storing liquid medications. HDPE pill bottles are opaque and have excellent chemical resistance, making them ideal for storing solid medications and protecting them from light exposure. Both PET and HDPE pill bottles are widely used in the pharmaceutical industry for their convenience, safety, and ability to preserve the quality of medications.</p></p>

<p><p>The bridge bearing pad market can be categorized into three types: neoprene, natural rubber, and others. Neoprene bearing pads are made of a synthetic rubber material that offers high resistance to heat, oils, and weathering, making them ideal for bridges in harsh environments. Natural rubber pads are made of a highly resilient material that provides excellent vibration isolation and energy absorption properties. The "others" category includes bearing pads made from materials such as polyurethane, laminated elastomers, and steel reinforced elastomers, providing a wider range of options for bridge construction projects.</p></p>

<p><p>The off-the-road tire market can be classified into three types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires contain compressed air, offering excellent traction and shock absorption, making them suitable for rough terrains. Solid tires are made of a solid rubber compound, eliminating the risk of punctures, but providing limited shock absorption capabilities. Polyurethane tires are a type of solid tire that offer better load-bearing capacity and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. These tire types cater to different needs by offering varying levels of traction, durability, and shock absorption.</p></p>

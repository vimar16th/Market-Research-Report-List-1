<p><p>The C-Reactive Protein Test Kits market offers various types of testing methods. Immunoturbidimetric kits utilize tiny particles that react with C-reactive protein, causing a change in turbidity, which can be measured and used for analysis. ELISA kits employ enzymes to produce measurable signals when C-reactive protein binds to specific antibodies. CLIA (Chemiluminescence Immunoassay) kits use chemicals that emit light when in contact with C-reactive protein, allowing for detection. Other kits encompass alternative testing methods for C-reactive protein, aiming to offer diverse options for diagnostic purposes within the market.</p></p>

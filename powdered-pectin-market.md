<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Powdered Pectin is categorized into three main types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin, and Amidated low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin. HM Pectin is derived from citrus peel and forms a gel in the presence of high sugar content and low pH. LMC Pectin requires calcium ions for gelling and is suitable for products with low sugar content and high pH. LMA Pectin is used for products that require strong and stable gels in a wide range of pH levels, making it versatile for various food applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The silica gel market comprises three major types: Type A, Type B, and Type C. Type A silica gel is highly porous, offering superior adsorption capacity at low humidity levels. It is commonly used in moisture control applications, including transportation and storage of goods. Type B silica gel, on the other hand, is designed specifically for high humidity environments, providing efficient adsorption in areas with high water vapor levels. Lastly, Type C silica gel combines the properties of both Type A and Type B, making it suitable for a wider range of humidity conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) Slurry is used in the semiconductor industry to achieve a smooth and flat surface during the manufacturing process. Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurry are the three major types of CMP slurries available in the market. Alumina Slurry consists of fine particles of aluminum oxide, which is effective for polishing metal and dielectric materials. Colloidal Silica Slurry contains nano-sized silicon dioxide particles that provide high removal rates and excellent selectivity. Ceria Slurry uses cerium oxide particles, suitable for polishing hard materials like glass and silicon carbide.</p></p>

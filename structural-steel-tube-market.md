<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The structural steel tube market is categorized into various types. Spiral weld tube is formed by continuously welding a steel strip in a spiral fashion, ensuring high strength and versatility. Double submerged arc weld (DSAW) tube is created by double submerged arc welding, offering excellent durability and larger diameters. Electric resistance weld (ERW) tube is produced through electric resistance welding, providing cost-effectiveness and a wide range of sizes. Seamless (SMLS) tube is manufactured without any welding, resulting in enhanced strength, smooth finish, and suitability for high-pressure applications.</p></p>

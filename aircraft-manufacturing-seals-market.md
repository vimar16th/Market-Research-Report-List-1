<p><ul><li>Static Aircraft Seals</li><li>Dynamic Aircraft Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Manufacturing Seals market consists of two main types: static aircraft seals and dynamic aircraft seals. Static aircraft seals are designed to prevent the flow of fluids or gases between stationary parts of an aircraft, providing a leak-free and airtight seal. On the other hand, dynamic aircraft seals are intended for moving parts, such as engines or landing gear, and are specifically engineered to handle continuous motion while maintaining an effective seal. Both types of seals play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and performance of aircraft during flight.</p></p>

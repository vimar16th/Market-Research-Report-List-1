<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alloy die castings market is classified into various types of casting processes, including pressure die casting, vacuum die casting, squeeze die casting, semi-solid die casting, and others. </p><p>Pressure die casting involves injecting molten alloy into a high-pressure mold to create the desired shape. Vacuum die casting utilizes a vacuum to extract air from the mold cavity before injecting the molten alloy. Squeeze die casting involves applying a high pressure on the mold to reduce porosity and improve material density. Semi-solid die casting involves using a partially solidified alloy to create components. Lastly, "others" encompass alternative die casting methods not mentioned above.</p></p>

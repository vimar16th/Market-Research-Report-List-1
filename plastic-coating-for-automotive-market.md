<p><p>In the automotive market, plastic coating is used to protect and enhance the exterior appearance of vehicles. The process involves applying multiple layers: primer, base coat, and clear coat. Primer is the first layer applied to the surface to ensure better adhesion of subsequent layers. The base coat provides color and texture to the coating, while the clear coat is the final layer that adds gloss and protects the underlying layers from weathering, scratches, and UV damage. Together, these layers create a durable and visually appealing finish for automobiles.</p></p>

<p><p>The hydraulic brake fluid market consists of several types, including DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is a standard type suitable for most vehicles with average braking systems. DOT 4 offers higher boiling points and better performance, making it ideal for heavier-duty vehicles or those with more demanding braking systems. DOT 5 is silicone-based and is primarily used in specialized applications, while DOT 5.1 is a high-performance fluid with similar properties to DOT 4, but with an even higher boiling point. Market demand for these brake fluids varies depending on the specific requirements of vehicles and brake systems.</p></p>

The increasing demand for automobiles, advancements in automation technology, and strict emission regulations are driving the market growth. This integration improves the performance and efficiency of automotive systems. For instance, electronic throttle control systems have replaced traditional mechanical linkage systems, enhancing the precision and response of throttle control.</p><p>Another trend is the growing adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) and hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs).

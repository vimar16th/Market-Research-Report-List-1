<p><p>Silicone for personal care market encompasses various types of silicone products. Silicone resin is a crosslinked silicone polymer used for its film-forming and water-resistant properties. Silicone gel is a translucent gel with excellent spreadability and softening effects, commonly used in skincare and haircare products.

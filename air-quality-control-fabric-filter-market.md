<p><p>The Air Quality Control Fabric Filter Market can be categorized into two types: Nanometer Level and Millimeter Level. The Nanometer Level refers to filters that are designed to capture particles with a size in the nanometer range, which is incredibly small and includes substances like bacteria and viruses. On the other hand, the Millimeter Level filters are designed to capture larger particles such as dust, pollen, and other particulate matter measuring in millimeters. Both types of filters serve the purpose of improving air quality but differ in their ability to capture particles of varying sizes.</p></p>

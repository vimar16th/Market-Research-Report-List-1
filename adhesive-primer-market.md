<p><p>Adhesive primer is a type of primer used to enhance the adhesion of adhesives or bonding materials to a wide range of surfaces. <p><p>Adhesive primers play a crucial role in ensuring strong adhesion between substrates and adhesives. Silane, Maleic Anhydride, Polyolefin, Titanate, and Zirconate are the different types of adhesive primers available in the market. Silane primers contain organosilanes and are widely used for bonding glass, metal, and other materials. Maleic Anhydride primers contain maleic anhydride copolymers and are commonly used for enhancing adhesion in plastic substrates. Polyolefin primers are based on polyolefin compounds and are suitable for improving bonding with polyolefin-based materials. Titanate and Zirconate primers are known for their excellent adhesion properties on various substrates, including metals and ceramics.</p></p>

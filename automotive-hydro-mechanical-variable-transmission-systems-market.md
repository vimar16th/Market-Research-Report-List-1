<p><p>The Automotive Hydro Mechanical Variable Transmission Systems market includes two main types: Mechanical Travel Drive and Hydrostatic Travel Drive. The Mechanical Travel Drive system uses planetary gears and clutches to vary the speed and torque transmission, providing a mechanically efficient solution for vehicles. On the other hand, the Hydrostatic Travel Drive system uses hydraulic components to control speed and torque, allowing for greater flexibility and precision in different operating conditions. Both systems play a significant role in the transmission technology used in automotive applications.</p></p>

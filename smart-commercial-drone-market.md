<p><p>The smart commercial drone market can be categorized into two main types: fixed-wing and rotary blade drones. Fixed-wing drones resemble traditional airplanes and are designed for long-range and high-speed operations. They are typically used in industries like agriculture and mapping, where large areas need to be covered. On the other hand, rotary blade drones, also known as quadcopters, have multiple rotors and offer vertical takeoff and landing capabilities. These drones are widely utilized in industries like photography, videography, and package deliveries, as they provide agility and precise maneuverability for close-range operations.</p></p>

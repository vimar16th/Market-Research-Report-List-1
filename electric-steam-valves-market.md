<p><p>The single-acting steam valve is a type of electric steam valve that operates with the help of only one direction of fluid pressure. It is typically used for applications where the fluid needs to be shut off or controlled in one direction only. On the other hand, the double-acting steam valve is another type of electric steam valve that operates with fluid pressure in both directions. This valve allows for bidirectional control of the fluid flow and is widely used in applications requiring greater control over the steam flow.</p></p>

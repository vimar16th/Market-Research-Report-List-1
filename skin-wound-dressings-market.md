<p><p>Skin wound dressings are used to promote healing and protect wounds from infection. Passive dressings are non-medicated and primarily provide a physical barrier to external elements. They are simple to use and cost-effective. On the other hand, bioactive dressings contain active ingredients like antimicrobials or growth factors that actively interact with the wound to accelerate healing. They are designed to enhance wound healing by stimulating cellular regeneration and controlling infection. Bioactive dressings are often more expensive but offer advanced therapeutic benefits.</p></p>
